Finding Missing Device Drivers

To locate additional Sun and third-party drivers and devices OpenSolaris, see the following resources:

  • Wireless drivers: http://www.opensolaris.org/os/community/laptop/wireless/

    Select the driver name to get a list of devices supported by the driver and to get download, installation, and configuration instructions.

  • OpenSolaris Device Drivers Community: http://www.opensolaris.org/os/community/device_drivers/

  • Open Source Software Sites on the Solaris Community: http://www.sun.com/software/solaris/community_resources.jsp

  • Third Party Products & Solutions: http://www.sun.com/io_technologies/

    Select the technology on the left side of the page, such as Ethernet or USB.

  • Hardware Drivers on the Sun Download Center: http://www.sun.com/download/

    Select the tab View by Category. Select the Hardware Drivers category.
